Technology Filmmaking and Photography Society, IIT Kharagpur

TFPS, as it's better known, is the official society of the institute for students interested in filmmaking, photography and film-appreciation, with a highly active presence on YouTube and Instagram.

Founded in 2010 by film enthusiasts Biswapati Sarkar, Ravi Sinha, Gaurav Kumar, Vasu Kamboj and Kaustubh Khare, TFPS has come a long way in promoting filmmaking and film appreciation among the campus fraternity as well as outside. It has also been a launchpad for students interested in pursuing careers in the entertainment industry, releasing a couple of national award-winning films too.
